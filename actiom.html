Rules of the Game

Each Actiom ID has a percentage Authenticity and Uniqueness score which indicates the ID's likelihood of representing a real human. The score attenuates all actiomarks associated with the ID. All Actiom IDs regularly generate the same amount of single-use actiomarks for the ID owner to send to other ID(s) on the Blockchain. The owner can split up his unsent points and void his sent points. Alias IDs exist to route actiomarks but all actiomarks will rest with Actiom IDs. After the owner dies, his ID stops generating points but can still receive points from other ID(s).
