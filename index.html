It is impossible to standardize a money-based Universal Basic Income across the globe.

Solution: A vote-based Minimum Income System: (pending project)
Upvoting with points is wide-spread online. In a unified system, we can send the same amount of points to each other every day. Points are single use; they cannot be re-spent like money. The senders can destroy the face values of their sent points. Points can be sent to a collection of people or an entity (aliases), but at the end, these points are always routed to verified individual accounts (real accounts).
